Risk Profile & Disclaimers

Understanding the risks associated with your investment is crucial.

Risk Profile: High

The Global Growth Equity Fund is classified as having a **High Risk Profile** due to its investment in equity markets.

Key Risks:

  • Market Risk: The value of investments may go down due to adverse movements in equity markets.
  • Concentration Risk: While diversified, higher exposure to certain sectors (e.g., Technology) or regions can increase sensitivity.
  • Liquidity Risk: Challenging to sell certain securities at desired prices under specific market conditions.
  • Currency Risk: Fluctuations in exchange rates may impact the value of overseas investments.
  • Growth Stock Volatility: Growth companies can be more sensitive to market sentiment, leading to higher price volatility.
